02 : About New Depths

The objective of this mission is to analyze the soil below the surface of Columbia Hills, which is believed to be a former hot spring. Chemical composition and pH data are to be obtained and compared to similar sites on Earth. Hardness of the soil will also be determined. This will better our understanding of what Columbia Hills is made up of underneath the surface, further our search of past life on Mars, and investigate the biological potential of the soil at Columbia Hills to support agriculture.
